My Data Science Bookshelf

Books I've Read

  • "Python for Data Analysis" by Wes McKinney - A great resource for learning data analysis with Python.
  • "Introduction to Data Science" by Jeffrey Stanton - An introductory book to the field of data science.
  • "Data Science for Business" by Foster Provost and Tom Fawcett - Explains the concepts of data science from a business perspective.

Books on My Reading List

  • "The Art of Data Science" by Roger D. Peng - A comprehensive guide to the art of data analysis.
  • "Data Science for Dummies" by Lillian Pierson - A beginner-friendly introduction to data science.
  • "Practical Statistics for Data Scientists" by Andrew Bruce and Peter Bruce - Covers practical statistics for data analysis.
