About me

Hey there, I'm Rama Wijaya, and I'm all about the data life! 📊 Based in Indonesia, I'm on a mission to turn raw data into meaningful insights.

With a degree in Animal Science and a solid GPA of 3.76/4.0, I've ventured into the world of data to satisfy my curiosity. You can call me a data detective, always on the hunt for patterns and stories hidden within numbers. 🕵️‍♂️

I'm pumped to tackle data challenges, work on cool projects, and make the data world a better place. Let's connect and turn data into something amazing!
